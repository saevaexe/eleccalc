Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my subscription?

Go to Settings → Restore Purchases in the app. Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID and will be restored automatically.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions to manage or cancel your ElecCalc subscription.

Which standards are the calculations based on?

ElecCalc follows IEC standards including:

  • IEC 60364-5-52 — Cable ampacity
  • IEC 60364-4-43 — Protection coordination
  • IEC 60034-30-1 — Motor efficiency classes

How do I delete my calculation history?

Open the History tab and tap the trash icon in the top-right corner to delete all records. You can also swipe individual entries to delete them.

Is my data shared with anyone?

No. All calculations are stored locally on your device only. We do not collect or share any personal data. See our Privacy Policy for details.
