ElecCalc

Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 8, 2026

ElecCalc ("the App") is developed by Osman Seven. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. ElecCalc is designed to work entirely on your device. Specifically:

  • No account registration is required.
  • No personal information (name, email, phone number) is collected.
  • No location data is accessed.
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used.
  • No cookies or similar technologies are employed.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Calculation History: Your past calculations are saved using Apple's SwiftData framework so you can review them later.
  • Favorites: Calculations you mark as favorites are stored locally.
  • App Preferences: Settings such as onboarding completion status are stored via UserDefaults.

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

3. Subscriptions

ElecCalc offers optional in-app subscriptions (monthly and yearly) managed entirely through Apple's App Store. Payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external service used is Apple's StoreKit for subscription management.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable content.

6. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data by:

  • Using the "Delete All" option in the History tab within the App.
  • Uninstalling the App from your device.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
